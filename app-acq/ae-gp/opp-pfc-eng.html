Process Flow Chart for the Completion of Green Procurement Plans

The entire process (from initial draft to posting on the web) takes approximately 6 weeks to complete. Please consider this in your planning.

ACRONYMS

  • CMPED – Commodity Management and Procurement Engineering Directorate, Acquisitions Branch
  • CT – Commodity Teams, Acquisition Branch
  • GPT – Green Procurement Team, Acquisitions Branch
  • EABD – Electronic Acquisition Business Directorate
  • CMC – Commodity Management Committee
  • Plan – Questionnaire + Scorecard
  • RFI – Request for Information
  • RFX – Request for Standing Offer or Request For Proposal

Expert advisors:

  • OGGO – Office of Greening of Government Operations
  • OSME – Office of Small and Medium Enterprises
  • EC – Environment Canada
  • NRCan – Natural Resources Canada

Green Procurement Plan Updates

Plans must be updated upon renewal of a procurement instrument. Plan updates follow the same process as outlined in the flowchart.
